Affordable Healthcare for Everyone

Join the Mukando Wehutano!

Santa Thadeu's Community Health Access Program helps people get good healthcare, no matter how much money they have.

What It Is

  • A community savings program for healthcare. Think of it like mukando, but for your health!

Why It Matters

  • Emergencies happen - be covered even when you're broke.

  • Prevention saves lives - get early checkups & treatment usually reserved for the wealthy.

  • Fair & dignified healthcare - access quality clinics without worry.

How It Works

  • Form a Group - Join with friends or family.

  • Contribute $20/month into your shared Health Pot.

  • Get Care When Needed - treatment at Santa Thadeu Clinics.

  • Refund - After 6 months, unused money is returned, just like an ordinary mukando.
